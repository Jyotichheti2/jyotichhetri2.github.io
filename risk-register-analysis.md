## Risk Assessment Framework
Following standard GRC practices, I evaluated risks based on a 1-3 scale for both likelihood and impact severity to calculate an overall Priority Score.

### Key Risks Identified:
* **Business Email Compromise (BEC):** Identified as a high-priority risk (Score: 6) where employees could be manipulated into sharing confidential data.
* **Physical Security:** Highlighted the risk of theft due to unsecured physical assets (Score: 6).
* **Data Integrity:** Analyzed the impact of poorly encrypted user databases and publicly accessible financial records.
* **Supply Chain:** Evaluated the likelihood of service disruptions due to natural disasters or delivery delays.

## Technical Skills & Concepts
* **Risk Quantification:** Calculating priority levels using the formula (Likelihood x Impact Severity).
* **Threat Identification:** Distinguishing between internal vulnerabilities (unlocked safes, poor encryption) and external threats (BEC, natural disasters).
* **Compliance Awareness:** Addressing strict financial regulations and Federal Reserve requirements for data and fund security.
